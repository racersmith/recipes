Ingredients:
 - 1 flour tortillia
 - 1/4c shreaded cheader cheese

Directions:
 1. Heat non-stick pan
 2. Place tortilla on pan and sprinkle with cheese
 3. Cook until cheese starts to melt
 4. Transfer tortillia to cutting board
 5. Fold tortillia in half
 6. Cut into 4 equal size wedges
 7. Transfer to plate
